What does PWA actually mean?

  • PWA, or Progressive Web Application is an application written in a web language (JavaScript + frameworks), which works as a native mobile or desktop application.

What are business benefits of PWA implementation?

What are business benefits of PWA implementation?

  • Cost optimization
  • Improved page performance
  • Increased user engagement

What are the features of PWA?

What are the features of PWA?

  • Installable on Homescreen
  • Offline support with service workers
  • Usage of various caching strategies
  • Web push notifications
  • Background sync
  • Native device features like camera
